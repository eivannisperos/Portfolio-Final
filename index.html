FLARE

FLARE

MAY 2016 - SEPTEMBER 2016

JAVA ANDROID-STUDIO

FLARE is a rapid response emergency application that allows the user to contact emergency services with a touch of a button. Emergency events can occur anywhere, even when the user himself/herself is incapacitated and cannot contact the proper authorities. FLARE connects users with emergency responders with as minimal keystrokes or interactions with the application to decrease the amount of time and effort it takes to contact them.
